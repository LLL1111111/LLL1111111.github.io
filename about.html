Personal introduction

I am interested in how technical constraints become product decisions, industry structure, and market consequences. My writing is usually somewhere between research memo, strategy note, and public notebook.

The recurring question behind this site is simple: where are the real bottlenecks moving, and what changes once people start building around them?
