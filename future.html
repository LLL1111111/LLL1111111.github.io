Future map

Tracking where the next constraints and opportunities move.

This page replaces the old research page with a forward-looking map. The goal is not just to list projects, but to keep a living view of trends, signals, open questions, and how different directions connect.

Core lens AI systems moving from capability demos to durable infrastructure
What changes Compute, data, memory, energy, agents, interfaces, and market structure
Output Trend maps, public notes, blog posts, and project-level analysis

Tracking
