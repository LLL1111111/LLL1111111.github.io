Agent Systems and Context Engineering

Why this matters — As agents take on longer, multi-step tasks, how context is built, edited, and retained becomes the dominant engineering problem, ahead of raw model capability.

Core questions

  • What does the emerging technical stack for context engineering look like?
  • How do context editing, agent design, and inference methods interact?
  • Where do current agent architectures break under long-horizon tasks?
